Olive Tapenade

This is a simple and delicious recipe for olive tapenade, a Proven√ßal spread.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pitted black olives
  • 2 tablespoons capers
  • 2 anchovy fillets
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Instructions

  1. Combine all ingredients in a food processor.
  2. Pulse until finely chopped but not pureed.
  3. Serve with crackers or sliced baguette.
